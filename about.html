About me

Hello everyone!

I'm PixelBoot! Since a very young age, I've been a passionate custom Android developer, recognised by XDA Developers for all the contribution to the community.

Goals

  • Creating an honest and helpful community on platforms like XDA and Telegram
  • Collaborating with users to gather feedback on issues and bugs for further stability
  • Upbringing stable Android versions on legacy devices to extend lifespan
  • Tackling devices specification issues, such as partition tables with newer Android upbrings
  • Ensuring stable usage on ROMs that are tested on actual hardware
  • Speaking to users to implement/suggest new features to existing custom ROMs.
