--- title: January 2021 Update ---

31 January 2021

Monthly Update (January 2021), is now available for Google Pixel and Pixel 2 devices.

Unfortunately, as for the Google Nexus 6P, official PixelExperience support has been dropped, as it was too early to produce an Android 11 build for the public.

The transition to Android 11 is well underway for supported devices.
