--- title: Android 13 arrives on the Google Pixel 2 devices ---

23 August 2022


Within a week of Android 13's release, I have been hard at work for Google Pixel devices for their Android 13 bringup.

Today I am happy to announce that the Google Pixel 2 devices have succesfully booted the latest version of Android thanks to ArrowOS.

Of course, given the short-term release of this build, you are expected to encounter bugs, most importantly:

  • 4G LTE/RIL
  • Permissive SELinux
  • You tell me

As mentioned previously, these builds are not released for daily use, and are used for enthusiasts who are willing to support my work by providing some feedback on their experience.

As always, if you encounter something new, feel free to use any of my links below to contact me either personally, or via the group conversations on Telegram that I take part in.

A point worth mentioning, is that I believe in community work, and as so, all of my work is public on Github.

If you are enthusiastic about this new (not daily driven release), get your hands on the build below, and feel free to get in touch.
