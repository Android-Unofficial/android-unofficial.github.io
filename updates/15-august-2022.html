--- title: Plans for Android 13 Development icon: android ---

15 August 2022

Android 13 is now live!


Android 13 has been released for Pixel 4/4XL, 4a, 4a (5G), 5, 6, 6 Pro, 6a devices, but what about the rest?


I'll be doing my part for the following devices to propose a possible upbring to the latest Android version:

  • Google Pixel 2/2 XL
  • Google Pixel 3/3 XL (with ReallySnow)
  • Google Pixel 3a/3a XL
  • Google Pixel 4/4 XL
  • Google Pixel 4a

This transition will be easier for the devices officially supported with Android 13 (4/4XL, 4a), however I do thank everyone's patience when it comes for the rest of the devices, especially with the introduction of the 3a devices.


On that note, if someone is willing to pledge in for the 3a devices, with some help to testing on my builds, or donate his/her personal device to help improve the rate at which Android 13 is brought up, feel free to contact me.


You'll be hearing from me soon with regards to how the process is going, and I hope to collaborate with you soon!
